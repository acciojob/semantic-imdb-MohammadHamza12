Summary

Interstellar is a science fiction film directed by Christopher Nolan. The story follows Cooper, a former NASA pilot, who embarks on a space mission with a team of scientists to find a new habitable planet as Earth faces ecological collapse. The movie explores time dilation, love, survival, and the human spirit across space and time.

Actors

The movie stars Matthew McConaughey as Cooper, Anne Hathaway as Dr. Amelia Brand, Jessica Chastain as Murphy, and Michael Caine as Professor Brand.
